Artist Connect

 Creative Collaboration

Rest Of My Family is a social-work-through-art initiative and bringing different artists and art-forms together to create socially relevant work is at the crux of our efforts.

The Forgotten Songs Collective (TFSC ) is the first project under Artist connect. TFSC is a multi-media art collective initiated by Rest Of My Family's Artist Connect programme in collaboration with vinayak^a that aims to preserve, raise awareness about dying tribal and folk music and cultures of India through multi-media artiste collaborations with musicians, visual artists, projection mapping artists, filmmakers and other artists.
