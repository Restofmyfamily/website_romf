#DriveForChange Campaign

As first campaign we drove non-stop for an entire year(Feb 2016-March 2017) through various rural and tribal areas. Living with, documenting the lives of and initiating development projects to empower various tribal and rural communities. We are currently in the process of post-producing various documentary films, short videos, photo stories etc.

Short Videos

These 1 to 3 minute videos, will be about the new experiences that ROMF have,meeting new faces, seeing new places. It could be a beautiful moment on the journey or story of a farmer, glimpse into a tribe’s culture and so on. It will be about the various socio-economic aspects of rural communities in India. In a month 3-4 such films are being released.

Interviews

Connecting with human beings is at the core of the journey and through interviews we get an insight into another person’s life, living conditions and views. This gives a voice to the hundreds of people that Piyush and Akshata meet. 2 or 3 interviews are being shared in a month

Documentary Films

These 20 to 30 minute long films would be social documentaries, each looking at a certain issue and ‘Rest Of My Family’s’ journey in understanding the issue, breaking it down, talking to people and experts, and sharing as they document the reality of the common people. Apart from these mini-documentary films, there will be a 90 minute full length documentary film bringing all these findings, the entire journey and its essence out.

Animation Clips

Short 1-2 minute animated clips will be used to convey our conclusions regarding various issues in a condensed, clearly spelt out fashion.

Photo Stories

Day-to-Day journals and Photo stories will be published regularly on our website, blog and with our partner publications like ‘The Hindu’.

Animation Explainer Videos

Short 2-5 minutes animated explainer videos are best to convey our conclusions regarding various issues in a condensed, clearly spelt out fashion. These can play a critical role in explaining in various social, psychological and philosophical issues facing our world and our existence today.

Multi-Media Collaborations

Through our Artist-Connect program we are continuously exploring ways and ideas with other collaborating artists that enable us to engage with society in new innovative ways.

As our first project in this direction we have collaborated with vinayak^a to revive ancient music of the Biate tribe of Assam

Rest Of My Family’ is a social-work-through-art initiative. Through the very design of our project, we aim to propagate a philosophy and a responsible way of looking at social issues through works of art. The aim is to use conventional and experimental modes of expression/art-forms- mix-media, multimedia art forms, documentary films, photo stories in order to bring about an engaging and long lasting social movement and transformation. With our efforts we wish to start a social movement towards a way of thinking that can bring about a long lasting transformation in the world. To achieve such a wide- spread and deep social penetration we need to use all creative mediums of expression in an efficient manner. By doing so we can ensure that we are making the most out of our work on ground.
